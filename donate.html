  • Established in 1978: Snehalayam Charitable Trust has been serving the community of Pattikkad, Thrissur since its establishment in 1978. Over the years, we have developed a strong presence and gained the trust of the local community.

  • Dedication to the community: Our primary focus is to bring love and support to the community of Pattikkad. We strive to understand and address the specific needs of the people living in this area, working tirelessly to improve their lives.

  • Clear mission: The core mission of Snehalayam Charitable Trust is to make a positive impact on the lives of individuals and families in need. Through our various programs and initiatives, we aim to provide aid, care, and support to those who require assistance.

  • Belief in the power of kindness: We firmly believe in the transformative power of kindness and compassion. By extending a helping hand and showing empathy towards those in need, we can create meaningful change and uplift our community as a whole.

Welcome to the Snehalayam Charitable Trust! Since 1978, we have been dedicated to bringing love and support to the community of Pattikkad, Thrissur. Our mission is clear: to make a positive impact by providing aid, care, and support to those in need. We believe in the power of kindness and the ability to create meaningful change when we come together. That's why we invite you to join us on this incredible journey of compassion. Your contribution can make a world of difference to the lives of those we serve. Every donation, no matter how big or small, helps us extend our reach and touch more lives. With your support, we can continue to provide essential resources, uplift individuals and families, and foster a brighter future for our community.
